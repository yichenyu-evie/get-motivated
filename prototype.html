Keep Motivated-Mobile APP Prototype

Tracker

Under the tracker section, users can check their habits that need to be finished today, and each habit will show how many days left in the streak. The gray habit means that habit is off today. Moreover, clicking on the plus button on the top-right can add a new habit.

Calendar

On the calendar, you can have a direct view of your overall completeness of your every day habits. If you complete all of your set goals that day, there will be a check mark under the corresponding date. If not, users can also click on it to see what he/she has missed and decide if he/she wants to use a buffer to make up.

Group Chat

The group chat page shows all the habit group you are in. The red dot on the left means the unread messages. Furthermore, you can start focus sessions in the group chat with your other habit mates. All the people in the same focus session are assumed doing the same habit and they can discuss in the focus synchronously.

User Center

The user can access the friends' list, achievements page, the in-app store, and settings in the user center. The friends' list contains all other users who the user has befriended in Motivation. The achievement and the redeem pages consist the reward system in this app. Lastly, users can change their personal information and app preferences in the settings.
